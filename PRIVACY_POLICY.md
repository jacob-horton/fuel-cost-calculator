## Privacy Policy

**Fuel Cost Calculator** was built with your privacy in mind.

### Our Privacy Promise:

- **We collect nothing:** We don't ask for your name, email, or any personal info.
- **We track nothing:** We don't use cookies, analytics, or trackers.
- **Stay Local:** All your trip data and fuel costs stay on your phone. Nothing is ever sent to a server.
- **No Third Parties:** We don't share data because we don't have any to share.

In short: what happens in the app, stays on your device.
